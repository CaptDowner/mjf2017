*Film: Chick Corea: The Musician

This is the portrait, in music and film, of a jazz freedom-fighter: his life, his art, his genius musical partners.

With this intimate documentary and four hours of live recordings, relive a now-legendary month of music.

Set in the epicenter of the jazz universe, New York City, capturing the essence of a master musician — piano player/composer/ bandleader: Armando Antonio “Chick” Corea.
